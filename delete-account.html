Delete Your Account

To delete your Winner Dinner account and all data associated with it, send a deletion request by email:

Request Account Deletion

Or email sami.prehn@gmail.com directly. Send the request from the Google account email you use to sign in so we can verify it's you.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, photo reference)
  • Your votes in all dinner parties
  • Your membership in all dinner parties
  • Your push notification device tokens

Dinner parties you created remain available to their other members, without your personal data. Deletion is completed within 30 days of the request.

Partial deletion

You can remove your data from a single dinner party without deleting your account — just leave the party in the app (trash icon on the home screen), which removes your votes and membership in it.
